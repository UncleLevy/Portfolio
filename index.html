About Me

Levy Bwalya Mulenga is a final-year student at Northrise University, where he is pursuing a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science with a focus on Cryptography. He is passionate about applying his knowledge and skills to solve real-world problems and create innovative solutions.

He has gained valuable experience in various roles, such as Club President at Innovators Arcade, where he led a team of 15 students to organize workshops, hackathons, and competitions on topics such as Arduino, LTSP, and Operating System Administration. He also worked as a computer technician at Roma Place Apartments, where he provided technical support and troubleshooting for over 100 residents, and as a waiter at Zebra Crossings Cafe, where he honed his customer service and communication skills.

Currently, he is working on a personal website to showcase his achievements and skills, and he has a forthcoming project focused on developing a Certificate Management System using Hashgraph, a novel distributed ledger technology that promises faster, cheaper, and more secure transactions. He is fluent in English, with native proficiency in Bemba and fluent in Nyanja, and he holds multiple certifications, including CompTIA A+, Introduction to Cybersecurity, and Avoiding Burnout. He is a goal-driven, hard-working, and detail-oriented individual, eager to contribute to team success and learn from others.
